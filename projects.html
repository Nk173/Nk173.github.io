Trade ABM
A modular, scalable agent-based model of international trade in which heterogeneous labourer agents across n countries and m sectors make labour, investment, and consumption decisions that drive endogenous production, prices, trade flows, and tariffs — extending Heckscher-Ohlin and New Trade Theory frameworks. The model is empirically calibrated against EORA26 MRIO trade data (USA, China, Mexico) using Nevergrad optimisation to match observed bilateral trade matrices.
