The Hindu BusinessLine Opinion · Op-Ed

It's unfair to NBFCs

August 7, 2019

On the case against using capital regulation as a blunt instrument for NBFCs. The piece argues that the higher capital adequacy norms imposed on non-deposit-taking NBFCs — relative to banks — violate institution neutrality and lock up funds that could otherwise be lent, when the real fix is a robust resolution framework rather than punitive capital requirements.
