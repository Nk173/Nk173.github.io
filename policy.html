A synthesis of two years of field research on where citizens fall out of India's cash-transfer pipeline — from registration and Aadhaar-seeding through to the last-mile cash-out — and what it implies for the design of inclusive welfare delivery.

Early-Stage Assessment of the Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana
Commissioned by the Ministry of Finance · micro-credit policy
Dvara Research, with IFMR LEAD · April 2018
An early-stage assessment of the Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana — the flagship micro-credit scheme — commissioned by the Ministry of Finance. It builds a theory-of-change framework for the scheme's components, evaluates its performance against stated objectives (finding it did not alter secular lending trends to target segments), and proposes a framework for monitoring intermediate effects and measuring long-term outcomes.
